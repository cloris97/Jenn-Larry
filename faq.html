Where are you located and when are the hours?

We are located at 49 York Street in Stratford Ontario, and we normally open from 12 - 5 pm on Saturdays. We are taking a break right now and we are closed, but our shop will re-open for the summer season.

How should I store my brittles?

Store the brittles in a completely dry, airtight container. Any plastic container or glass jar with a tight-fitting lid isfine. Ziplock plastic storage bags work, too.

How do I order?

You can come in to our physical store or call our number at 519-508-4949 to place an order. Once the order is ready, you can come in to pick up your dessert! We also have ready to go desserts in our store. If you want to order brittle, please place your order two days in advance.

Do you do delivery?

Unfortunately, at this time, we do not to delivery.

What are your most popular items?

Our most popular items are the ice cream and milkshakes, and our specialty is peanut brittle!

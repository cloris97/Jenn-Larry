Our Story

JENN’s father Larry and her mother, Gail, once owned and operated Larry and Gail's Drive-In from 1967-1974. Larry is famous for making delicious, hand-spun milkshakes. JENN founded Jenny's Peanut Brittle in 1999, and it has quickly become the best brittle in Stratford! Jenny had outgrown her kitchen, and needed a new location to create her popular brittle.

So...

The father and daughter duo went for it, opening JENN and Larry's Brittle’n Shakes & Ice Cream Cakes, a quaint little shop with a retro feel, on September 3, 2010, at 49 York Street, along the picturesque Avon River in Stratford, Ontario. We are proud to operate year-round, with a short break after Christmas to catch our breath, and now are so pleased to offer online ordering for your convenience. We look forward to serving you soon!
